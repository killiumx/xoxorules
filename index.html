📜 General Rules

Important: The server is not liable for any adult content, language, or interactions you may encounter.
  • DO NOT DM staff for support or server-related inquiries. Instead, open a support ticket so the staff team can assist effectively. If you are in-game, use /report to contact an admin.
  • Consistent rule-breaking or toxic behavior will result in a ban, at the discretion of the staff.
  • Respect the community and other players. Trolling or intentionally harassing others in-character is prohibited.
  • Zero tolerance: Harassment, racism, sexism, toxicity, "must-win" attitudes, or any form of hate speech is strictly prohibited. This applies across Discord, in-game interactions, and players' personal streams.
  • The use of slurs, including any variation of the "N" and "R" words, is a zero-tolerance offense.
  • Be considerate of others' roleplay! The goal is to create immersive and enjoyable scenarios for all players.
  • Using third-party voice communication (e.g., Discord, private calls) while in FiveM is strictly prohibited. All in-character communication must take place in the server.
  • Loophole exploitation is strictly prohibited. If you attempt to work around the rules in bad faith, staff will review the situation and determine the appropriate action.
  • Exploiting game mechanics or using bugs for personal gain is a bannable offense. Report any discovered exploits to the developers immediately.
  • No griefing. Continually targeting or attacking a player after a previous RP resolution is considered harassment.
  • If you witness a rule break, do not break character. Complete the scenario first, then submit a player report ticket.
  • Ban evasion is strictly prohibited. If you have been banned, ask a staff member to create a ban appeal ticket.

🚫 Zero Tolerance for Disrespect Toward Women

xoxo RP prioritizes an inclusive and respectful roleplay environment. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior targeting women will result in immediate action.

  • ✔️ No Verbal Harassment - No offensive or degrading remarks
  • ✔️ No disrespectful language towards female players
  • ✔️ No Inappropriate Behavior - No unwanted advances or suggestive jokes
  • ✔️ No RP scenarios that demean women
  • ✔️ Consent is required for sensitive RP topics

⚠️ Fail RP & Roleplay Guidelines

Meta Gaming (First-Offense Ban)

Meta gaming is using OOC (out-of-character) knowledge to influence in-game decisions. Examples include:

  • Watching live streams or VODs to gain knowledge about characters or locations.
  • Using information from Discord OOC chats in-game.

Power Gaming (Strictly Prohibited)

Power gaming is using unrealistic methods to gain an advantage in RP scenarios. Examples include:

  • Abusing game mechanics or exploits for unfair advantages.
  • Hiding in a house/apartment instance to avoid an active roleplay situation.
  • Using unrelated characters from different gangs, groups, or investigations to benefit your primary character.
  • Logging out before a scenario concludes, especially when placed in a 48-hour coma to avoid handing over items.

Combat-Related Fail RP

  • RDM (Random Deathmatch) & VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) are strictly prohibited.
  • Running over or shooting a player without proper roleplay buildup is considered Fail RP.
  • Hitman RP must be properly roleplayed. The target must have some knowledge that this may be happening.
  • Running people over in any instance = VDM.
  • All conflict must be initiated verbally. Attacking without proper communication is Fail RP.
  • Combat logging is prohibited. If your game crashes, you are required to return and finish the scenario.
  • Misusing player-based menus for IC (in-character) advantages is prohibited.
  • Eavesdropping through walls is not allowed. Players can only use information if they are physically present in the same room or area.
  • Pre-combat communication is required. Before a gunfight or attack, you must use phone messages, LifeInvader, Birdy, or other means of IC communication.

Additional RP Guidelines

  • Forgery of medical/legal documents for court RP is power gaming and not allowed.
  • All injuries, medical documents, and legal evidence must be roleplayed at the time of the incident.
  • Do not interfere with other players' RP unless you have a valid reason.
  • Do not perform unrealistic actions (dropping items while handcuffed, using a phone/radio while tied up, stacking more than two people on a motorcycle).

💬 Discord & Communication Rules

Discord Guidelines

  • Players must set their Discord nickname to match their in-game character name(s).
  • Toxicity, drama, or harassment in OOC chat is prohibited.
  • DO NOT DM staff members for support or inquiries! Always use the appropriate ticket system.

Voice Communication Rules

  • IC (In-Character) conversations must be in-game.
  • If you're interacting with someone in-game, mute/deafen yourself in Discord to maintain RP immersion.
  • Third-party voice chats (Discord calls, Teamspeak, etc.) for IC communication are prohibited.

Radio & Phone Use Rules

  • Radios cannot be used while handcuffed or otherwise restrained.
  • Phones cannot be accessed while tied up or unconscious.

OOC Chat Misuse

  • Do not spam OOC chat or use it for IC purposes.
  • OOC chat is only for brief clarifications, not extended discussions.

💰 Robberies

  • Players can only be robbed of belongings and money on their person, not from their bank account.
  • No pocket wipes: You cannot steal every item from a player's inventory. Stolen items must make sense for roleplay.
  • Players must be held up (hands up) to be robbed.
  • Camping job sites to steal materials is prohibited, but kidnapping from job sites is allowed.
  • Only the criminals who initiated the robbery can participate. No external help is permitted.
  • Motorcycles may not be parked inside buildings.
  • Criminals can only use motorcycles during crimes if at least three police officers are on duty.
  • Using a personal vehicle in a robbery results in higher impound fees and a 1-4 hour hold on the vehicle.

Kidnappings & Hostages

  • Police officers can only be kidnapped if four or more officers are on duty.
  • Civilians can be kidnapped even if no police are online.
  • Hostages cannot be held for more than 20 minutes unless all parties agree to an extended RP scenario.
  • Repeatedly robbing or kidnapping the same player is considered griefing.
  • Hostages cannot be carried on motorcycles.
  • Hostages must be random players—no pre-arranged scenarios with friends. Violations will result in punishment.

ATM & Bank Robberies

  • ATM Robberies: If conducting with multiple people, a seated vehicle (not bikes) is required.
  • No abandoning an ATM robbery to start another—this is an exploit.
  • Bank Heists (FLEECA, Big Bank, Paleto, etc.) require hostages—no negotiations without one.
  • Solo robberies (houses, stores, ATMs, jewelry stores) do not require hostages.
  • Hostages must be random—no staged scenarios with friends or associates.

Hostage Rules

  • Hostages must be held at gunpoint at all times. If unguarded, police may retrieve them without negotiation.
  • No holding hostages during robbery cooldowns—release them and leave the area.
  • Proof of life must be provided to law enforcement in person.

🚓 Police-Specific Rules

  • Officers should issue tickets to players loitering excessively in one location.
  • Without bodycam footage, officers are treated as civilians in reports—no evidence means admin discretion applies.
  • Officers entering an active gang battle event may be shot on sight.

Corruption Rules

  • Low-level corruption (small bribes) is only allowed for officers below Sergeant rank.
  • No corruption is permitted for on-duty EMS or police.
  • Selling police-issued weapons, tools, or evidence is strictly forbidden and results in an OOC strike and in-character termination.

Interacting with Police

Roleplay (RP) is mandatory once PD is present.
  • Treat RP as you would like to receive it.
  • Not every encounter should turn into a high-speed chase. Stop when required.
  • If you break out of cuffs or escape, you are allowed to shoot.
  • Police aiming a lethal weapon at you is initiation. (Tasers are not lethal.)
  • After two taser attempts, you are allowed to shoot at PD.
  • If you intend to shoot at PD, you must give them a chance to pull their weapons out.

Police Actions

  • Criminals Pointing Guns: If you point a gun at a police officer or another person – you have initiated conflict and police can shoot you.
  • Shooting Tires: Police can shoot vehicle tires if the vehicle is about to be used in an escape attempt.
  • Hostage Situations: If a hostage taker does not give clear demands, police must act to save the hostage with lethal force.
  • Stealing Police Property: Attempting to steal a police cruiser or weapon = you can be shot.
  • Spike Strips: Police can use spike strips in pursuits. Vehicles can be preemptively spiked if parked illegally & confirmed to be used in a crime.

Warrants, Searches & Arrests

  • If you committed a felony or have a warrant, police can enter your owned property.
  • Vehicle searches require no warrant—only probable cause needed.
  • Not being read your rights = arrest still valid, but any questioning done without Miranda cannot be used in court.

🔫 Criminal-Specific Rules

  • No camping or claiming drug labs, money laundering spots, or black market locations.
  • No targeting EMS for doing their jobs (e.g., reporting injured criminals). Personal conflicts are allowed.
  • No hiding in garages/apartments to evade police chases—this results in a strike.
  • Cop baiting (harassing officers or forcing chases) is prohibited.
  • /scene cannot be used as graffiti—only spray cans are allowed for tagging.
  • No vulgar or hate speech via /scene.
  • Shooting at public officials (EMS, police) increases maximum jail time to 20 months at the officer's discretion.
  • Criminals must fear police presence—no lingering at crime scenes after a warning.

Returning to RP Scenes

  • Players cannot return to a previous RP scene once they have fully left it.
  • If police are downed and being rescued, criminals cannot return to re-engage.
  • If downed and receiving medical RP, you are out of the scenario and cannot rejoin.
  • Clearing the scene means traveling more than one block away and breaking line of sight without being followed.

🚗 Vehicle Rules

  • Players cannot steal EMS or police vehicles.
  • Police-issued weapons cannot be robbed from officers, but criminals may keep weapons taken from other criminals.
  • If police successfully pit and box-in a criminal's vehicle, the getaway car must stop. Criminals may flee on foot, carjack another vehicle, or initiate a shootout.
  • Criminals cannot ram their way out of a police box-in.

Police Evasion Rules

  • You cannot evade police without a valid reason (e.g., an active warrant). Speeding alone is not justification.
  • No outside help is allowed—only those originally involved in the crime may participate in the chase.
  • Drive realistically—no off-roading in supercars, ramp jumps, or unrealistic maneuvers.

1/1 Vehicle Transfer Rules

  • All 1/1 vehicles are permanently bound to their original purchaser and cannot be sold, traded, or gifted under normal circumstances.
  • Exception: If you wish to transfer ownership of a 1/1 vehicle, you must submit a transfer request ticket in the server's Discord.

🟢 Green-Zones & Safe Zones

Warning: Killing inside a green-zone results in a character wipe.
  • Green-zones are always in-character—no OOC exceptions.
  • No green-zone camping.
  • No stealing/robbing vehicles in green-zones.
  • Safe-zones are for social interactions and passive RP only—no loitering.
  • No violence near safe-zones—robberies must be far away to prevent abuse.

List of Green-Zone Locations

  • Business locations (may vary)
  • Hospitals (2-block radius)
  • Mall
  • Clothing stores & parking lots (instant 24-hour ban for violence)
  • Gun stores & parking lots
  • All event zones (if posted in Discord)
  • Casino
  • Crafting benches (no camping or robbing)
  • PD impound lot

🎥 CLIPPING RULES

No clip, no sit – proof is REQUIRED for reports.
  • Clipping software is required—you MUST have recording software installed (e.g., Medal.tv or GeForce Experience).
  • All clips must include audio from both parties.
  • Your microphone audio must be included.
  • Reports without full audio will be voided.
  • Clips must be at least 1-2 minutes long (or longer).

👕 CLOTHING RULES

  • Bulletproof helmets are not allowed due to unfair advantage.
  • Gang clothing (bandanas, flags, etc.) may only be worn by actual gang members.
  • Police/EMS clothing (vests, hats, belts, shirts) may only be worn by those in those roles.
  • Character model glitches (missing arms, half arms, etc.) are not permitted.

⚖️ Admin Sit Rules

  • Do not interfere in another player's admin sit—doing so may result in punishment.
  • Only the reporting player should speak when an admin is handling the case.
  • Follow admin instructions immediately—failure to comply will result in penalties.
  • Video evidence is required for reports—submit clips via Discord tickets.
  • Disrespecting admins will lead to bans or jail time.
  • Spamming /reports will result in a kick, then a ban if repeated.
  • Admins may confiscate illegal items if rules are broken.

💵 Money & Anti-Cheat Rules

  • Always deposit cash in the bank—money lost in pockets or vehicles will not be refunded.
  • Carrying large amounts of cash (clean or dirty) increases the risk of anti-cheat deletion.
  • Only bank-stored money is eligible for reimbursement.

1/1 Firearm Rules

  • Only the original purchaser may own a 1/1 gun—unauthorized possession is punishable.
  • Selling, gifting, or transferring 1/1 guns is strictly prohibited.
  • To verify ownership, submit a clear photo of the gun via a Discord ticket.
  • All should have a WL from CPD.
  • Do not submit tickets for broken or depleted weapons. Repairs must be handled in-character (RP) through gun repair services.
