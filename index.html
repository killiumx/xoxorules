🌟 Community Standards & Conduct

⚠️ Legal Disclaimer

By joining XOXO RP, you acknowledge that mature content, language, and themes may be present. The server administration is not responsible for player interactions or content encountered during gameplay.

Core Community Principles

  • Support Protocol: Never contact staff members directly via DMs. Submit a ticket through our support system or use the in-game /report command for immediate assistance.
  • Behavioral Standards: Repeated violations or disruptive behavior will result in removal from the community at staff discretion.
  • Mutual Respect: Treat all community members with dignity. Griefing, targeted harassment, and toxic behavior are prohibited.
  • Immersion Priority: Always consider how your actions affect other players' roleplay experiences. Quality RP benefits everyone.
  • Communication Guidelines: External voice platforms (Discord calls, etc.) are forbidden during active gameplay. All IC communication must occur in-server.
  • Rule Integrity: Attempting to bypass or exploit loopholes in server rules is prohibited and will be addressed by staff.
  • Fair Play: Exploiting bugs or game mechanics for personal advantage results in immediate removal. Report all bugs to development.
  • Conflict Resolution: Repeatedly targeting the same player after previous encounters have concluded constitutes harassment.
  • Reporting Violations: Never break character to address rule violations. Complete the scenario, then file a report.
  • Account Integrity: Ban evasion through alternate accounts is strictly forbidden. Appeal bans through proper channels.

🚫 Zero Tolerance Policy

XOXO RP maintains a zero-tolerance policy for the following:

  • ✖️ Discriminatory language or slurs of any kind
  • ✖️ Harassment, bullying, or targeted toxicity
  • ✖️ Sexist, racist, or homophobic behavior
  • ✖️ "Win at all costs" mentality that ruins RP quality
  • ✖️ Out-of-character drama or conflicts

This policy applies to: Discord communications, in-game interactions, personal streams, and all community platforms.

💜 Respect & Inclusivity Standard

XOXO RP is committed to maintaining a welcoming environment for all players. Any form of disrespect, inappropriate conduct, or harassment toward any community member will result in immediate disciplinary action. We prioritize creating safe, inclusive roleplay experiences where everyone feels valued.

🎭 Roleplay Quality Standards

Meta Gaming (Immediate Ban)

Using out-of-character information to influence in-character decisions is strictly prohibited.

  • No using Twitch streams or recordings to track player locations or activities
  • No referencing Discord conversations or out-of-game communications in RP
  • No sharing character information across different characters you control

Power Gaming (Severe Offense)

Manipulating game mechanics or scenarios to gain unfair advantages destroys RP quality.

  • Exploiting bugs or glitches for competitive advantage
  • Using properties or instances to escape active RP scenarios
  • Transferring assets or information between unrelated characters
  • Logging out during active scenarios to avoid consequences (especially 48-hour incapacitation)

Combat Engagement Standards

  • Initiation Required: RDM (Random Deathmatch) and VDM (Vehicular Deathmatch) are absolutely forbidden.
  • Context Matters: All violence must have proper roleplay buildup and justification.
  • Contract RP: Assassination scenarios require the target to have reasonable suspicion something may occur.
  • Vehicle Attacks: Using vehicles as weapons without proper context equals VDM.
  • Verbal Initiation: All hostile actions must begin with verbal communication or threats.
  • Connection Issues: If disconnected during a scenario, you must return immediately upon reconnecting.
  • Menu Abuse: Using player menus or commands to gain advantages during confrontations is prohibited.
  • Information Gathering: You cannot "listen through walls" - only information from your physical location is valid.
  • Pre-Conflict Warning: Before major confrontations, establish contact through phones, social media, or other IC methods.

Additional RP Standards

  • Only involve yourself in others' RP when you have legitimate IC reasons
  • Physical limitations apply: no dropping items while restrained, no phone use while incapacitated, maximum two passengers on motorcycles

📡 Communication Standards

Platform Guidelines

  • Keep out-of-character channels drama-free and respectful
  • Never DM staff directly - Always use the ticket system for support requests

In-Game Communication

  • All character interactions must happen in-game only
  • Mute Discord when engaging in roleplay to maintain immersion

Equipment Usage During RP

  • Radios are inaccessible while restrained or handcuffed
  • Mobile phones cannot be used when tied up or unconscious

Out-of-Character Chat Etiquette

  • OOC chat is for quick clarifications only, not extended conversations
  • Never spam or misuse OOC for in-character purposes

💰 Criminal Activity Guidelines

Robbery Standards

  • Players may only be robbed of cash and items they're physically carrying - bank accounts are protected
  • Selective Theft: Taking every item from someone's inventory is prohibited. Only take items that make sense contextually
  • Victims must have their hands raised before robbery can occur
  • Camping at job locations to steal materials is forbidden (kidnapping workers is allowed)
  • Only the original group who started the heist may participate - no reinforcements
  • Motorcycles cannot be stored inside buildings
  • Motorcycles can only be used in crimes when 3+ police officers are active
  • Using personal vehicles in heists results in extended impound times (1-4 hours) and higher fees

Hostage Situations & Kidnappings

  • Officers can only be taken hostage when 4+ officers are on duty
  • Civilians can be kidnapped regardless of police presence
  • Maximum hostage duration: 20 minutes (unless all parties agree to continue)
  • Repeatedly targeting the same victim is considered griefing
  • Hostages cannot ride on motorcycles
  • Random Selection Required: Hostages must be randomly selected strangers - using friends or associates will result in punishment

Bank & Store Heists

  • ATM heists with multiple participants require a car with seats (no motorcycles)
  • Abandoning one ATM robbery to start another is considered an exploit
  • Major heists (banks, Fleeca, Paleto) require a hostage for negotiations
  • Solo crimes (houses, convenience stores, ATMs, jewelry stores) don't need hostages
  • All hostages must be strangers - no staged scenarios with known associates

Hostage Management Protocol

  • Hostages must be guarded at gunpoint constantly - unguarded hostages can be rescued without negotiation
  • Release hostages immediately during robbery cooldown periods
  • Provide proof of life to law enforcement in person when requested

Criminal Behavior Standards

  • No claiming territory at drug processing, money laundering, or black market sites
  • EMS personnel are off-limits while performing duties (personal conflicts are separate)
  • Using garages or properties to escape police pursuit results in strikes
  • Cop baiting (provoking officers unnecessarily) is prohibited
  • /scene command is not for graffiti - only spray cans can be used for tagging
  • No offensive or hateful content via /scene commands
  • Attacking public servants (EMS, police) can extend sentences to 20 months
  • Criminals must respect police authority - no loitering at crime scenes after warnings

Scene Return Policy

  • Cannot return to a roleplay scene after you've completely left
  • If police are down and being revived, criminals cannot re-engage
  • Receiving medical treatment removes you from the scenario permanently
  • "Clear" means traveling more than one block away with broken line of sight

👮 Law Enforcement Protocols

Police Conduct

  • Issue citations to individuals excessively loitering in public spaces
  • Bodycam footage is required for reports - without it, officers are treated as civilians
  • Officers entering active gang conflict zones may be engaged immediately

Corruption Limitations

  • Minor corruption (small bribes) is only permitted for officers below Sergeant rank
  • On-duty EMS and police cannot engage in corrupt activities
  • Selling department equipment, weapons, or evidence results in immediate termination and OOC discipline

Engagement with Law Enforcement

🎭 Roleplay is Mandatory

Once police arrive, quality roleplay is required from all parties involved.

  • Engage with situations realistically - not every encounter should become a chase
  • Comply with lawful orders when appropriate for the scenario
  • Breaking free from restraints or escaping custody authorizes lethal force
  • Officers aiming lethal weapons at you constitutes threat initiation (tasers do not count)
  • After two failed taser attempts, you may engage with lethal force
  • If choosing to shoot at officers, you must give them time to draw weapons

Use of Force Authorization

  • Weapon Brandishing: Pointing firearms at officers or civilians justifies lethal response
  • Tire Deflation: Officers may shoot tires of vehicles being used for escape
  • Hostage Rescue: If demands are unclear or unreasonable, police must act to save hostages
  • Property Theft: Attempting to steal police vehicles or equipment authorizes shooting
  • Pursuit Tools: Spike strips are authorized in chases and can be pre-deployed at illegally parked crime vehicles

Searches, Warrants & Arrests

  • Active felony warrants authorize entry into owned properties
  • Vehicle searches only require probable cause - no warrant needed
  • Miranda rights must be read - statements without them are inadmissible in court, but arrest remains valid

🚗 Vehicle Standards & Pursuit Rules

Vehicle Protection

  • Emergency vehicles (EMS and police) cannot be stolen under any circumstances
  • Department-issued weapons stay with officers - criminals keep weapons taken from other criminals
  • When police successfully box in a vehicle, criminals must stop - they may flee on foot, steal another vehicle, or engage in combat
  • Ramming through police barriers is prohibited

Pursuit Engagement Rules

  • Valid reason required to flee police (active warrant, contraband, etc.) - speeding alone isn't justification
  • No outside assistance - only those involved in the original crime can participate
  • Drive realistically - no off-roading sports cars, ramp jumps, or impossible maneuvers

Exclusive Vehicle Transfers

  • One-of-one (1/1) vehicles are permanently bound to the original purchaser
  • These vehicles cannot be sold, traded, or given away normally
  • Exception: Submit a transfer request ticket via Discord for special circumstances

🟢 Protected Zones & Territories

⚠️ Critical Warning

Killing anyone inside a designated safe zone results in complete character deletion.

Safe Zone Guidelines

  • All safe zones remain in-character - no OOC exemptions exist
  • Camping or excessive loitering in safe zones is prohibited
  • No vehicle theft or robberies within safe zone boundaries
  • These areas are for social interaction and passive roleplay only
  • Criminal activity must occur far from safe zone borders to prevent exploitation

Designated Protected Locations

  • All commercial business locations (varies by ownership)
  • Medical facilities and 2-block surrounding radius
  • Shopping center
  • Clothing boutiques and adjacent parking areas (24-hour ban for violence)
  • Firearm retailers and parking zones
  • Community event locations (announced in Discord)
  • Entertainment venue
  • Crafting stations (no camping or theft)
  • Police impound facility

🎥 Evidence & Recording Requirements

📹 Recording Mandate

No evidence = No case. Video proof is mandatory for all player reports.

Recording Standards

  • Recording software is mandatory (Medal.tv, GeForce Experience, OBS, etc.)
  • All clips must include clear audio from both sides of the interaction
  • Your microphone audio must be captured and audible
  • Reports lacking complete audio will be dismissed
  • Minimum clip duration: 1-2 minutes (longer for complex situations)

👗 Character Appearance Guidelines

Clothing Restrictions

  • Bulletproof helmets are banned due to gameplay imbalance
  • Organization-specific attire (bandanas, colors, insignia) is restricted to verified members only
  • Law enforcement and medical uniforms are role-exclusive
  • Character model bugs (missing limbs, transparency glitches) must be fixed immediately

⚖️ Administrative Procedures

Staff Interaction Protocol

  • Never interfere in another player's administrative situation - doing so may result in your own punishment
  • Only the reporting party should speak during case reviews
  • Follow all staff instructions immediately and completely
  • Video evidence is required - submit through Discord ticket system
  • Disrespecting staff members results in bans or extended jail sentences
  • Spamming /report commands will result in kicks, then bans if continued
  • Staff may confiscate contraband obtained through rule violations

💵 Financial Protection & Asset Management

Money Security

  • Always deposit funds in your bank account - cash carried is at your own risk
  • Large cash amounts (clean or dirty) risk being flagged by anti-cheat systems
  • Only banked funds are eligible for administrative reimbursement

Exclusive Firearm Ownership

  • One-of-one (1/1) firearms are permanently bound to the original purchaser
  • Unauthorized possession of someone else's 1/1 weapon is a bannable offense
  • Selling, gifting, or transferring 1/1 weapons is absolutely forbidden
  • Ownership verification requires submitting clear photos through Discord tickets
  • All 1/1 weapons require CPD whitelisting authorization
  • Broken or depleted weapons must be repaired through in-character services - no administrative assistance
